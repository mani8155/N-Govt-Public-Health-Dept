You can upload the documents through the official Public Health Department website under the "Proforma Menu" section. Ensure each document is in the required format (PDF, JPG, PNG) and meets the size limit (maximum 10MB per file).

Only documents in English or Tamil will be accepted. If your documents are in any other language, you will need to provide a translation.

After submitting the documents, you will receive an acknowledgment email confirming your submission. The department will review your application, and you will be notified once the approval process is complete (usually within 7-10 business days).

Yes, there may be an administrative fee for document submission and processing. The payment details and fee structure will be provided on the official Public Health Department website, under the "Payment Details" section.

Payments can be made online through the official payment gateway on the Public Health Department website. Accepted payment methods include debit/credit cards, net banking, and UPI. Please ensure that you complete the payment before submitting the documents for approval.

The payment amount varies depending on the type of institution and the specific program you are applying for. For detailed information, please visit the "Payment Details" section on the website or contact the department directly.

Yes, there are specific deadlines for document submission, which are typically mentioned in the guidelines for each program. Ensure that you submit your documents before the specified deadline to avoid delays in approval.

Once your documents are reviewed and approved, you will receive an official approval notification through email, along with instructions on the next steps.

If your documents are rejected, you will be informed about the reasons for rejection. You will have the opportunity to correct the issues and resubmit the required documents.

If you encounter technical issues, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact the technical support team at support@health.tn.gov.in or call the helpline number provided on the website.

Once you submit your documents, no further changes can be made. If you realize that you've submitted incorrect information, please contact the Public Health Department as soon as possible, and they will guide you through the process for making amendments.

The approval process generally takes 7-10 business days after the submission and payment. However, in some cases, it may take longer depending on the volume of applications or if additional information is required.
