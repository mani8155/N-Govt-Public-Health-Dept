Help Page: Guide for Uploading Documents:

Follow these steps to upload your documents:

  1. Ensure all your documents are ready in PDF, JPG, or PNG format.
  2. Make sure that no file exceeds 10MB in size.
  3. Log in to the official application portal and access the "Document Upload" section.
  4. Click on the "Upload" button for each document type and select the file from your device.
  5. After uploading each document, click "Submit" to confirm the upload.
  6. Once all documents are uploaded, review your entries before submitting the application.

If you face any issues during the upload process, you may contact our help desk for assistance.
