Help Page: Public Health Department - Tamil Nadu

This page provides guidance to Institutes of Nursing, AMN, and Medical Colleges for collaboration with Public Health Centers and uploading mandatory documents for approval.

1. Introduction

The Public Health Department of Tamil Nadu collaborates with various institutes including nursing schools, AMN programs, and medical colleges. Institutions are required to submit mandatory documents for approval to participate in programs associated with the Public Health Centers.

2. Who Should Apply?

Institutions eligible for applying include:

  • Nursing Institutes
  • AMN (Auxiliary Nurse Midwife) Institutes
  • Medical Colleges
  • Other health-related institutions wishing to collaborate with Public Health Centers
3. Mandatory Documents

Before submitting an application, ensure the following mandatory documents are ready for upload:

  • Institute Registration Certificate
  • Accreditation Certificate from the Government/Medical Council
  • List of Faculty and Staff (including qualifications and experience)
  • Infrastructure and Facilities Verification Report
  • Annual Performance Report (if applicable)
  • Public Health Program Participation Record
  • Any other documents as per the Public Health Department guidelines
4. Step-by-Step Guide to Upload Documents

Follow these steps to upload your documents:

  1. Step 1: Visit the official Public Health Department website for Tamil Nadu.
  2. Step 2: Register/login to the system using your institution credentials.
  3. Step 3: Navigate to the "Documents Submission" section.
  4. Step 4: Upload all mandatory documents listed above. Ensure each file is in the required format (PDF, JPG, PNG, etc.).
  5. Step 5: After uploading, review the documents for accuracy.
  6. Step 6: Submit the application for approval.
  7. Step 7: You will receive an acknowledgment upon successful submission. Your application will be processed, and you will be notified once approved.
5. Common Issues and Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues during the application process, here are some common problems and solutions:

  • Problem: Unable to upload the document.
    Solution: Ensure the document is in the supported format (PDF, JPG, PNG) and does not exceed the file size limit of 10MB.
  • Problem: System error during document submission.
    Solution: Try clearing your browser cache or use a different browser. If the issue persists, contact the helpline.
  • Problem: Document submission status not updating.
    Solution: Wait for 24-48 hours for processing. If the issue persists, contact support for further assistance.
6. Contact Information

If you need further assistance, please feel free to reach out:

We hope this guide helps you successfully complete the document submission process for approval. Thank you for your cooperation in improving the public health system in Tamil Nadu.
