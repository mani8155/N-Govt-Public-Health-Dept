Help Page: Mandatory Documents:

The following documents are required for the application process:

  • College Affiliation Certificate from the relevant educational authority
  • Accreditation Certificate from the Medical/Nursing Council or equivalent body
  • Details of faculty, infrastructure, and other resources at the institution
  • Previous record of collaboration with healthcare facilities (if applicable)
  • Valid Registration Certificate of the institution
  • Letter of Intent (LOI) for tie-up with Tamil Nadu Public Health Centers
  • Payment Proof (Receipt of application fee)

All documents must be provided in clear and legible formats. Incomplete applications will not be processed.
