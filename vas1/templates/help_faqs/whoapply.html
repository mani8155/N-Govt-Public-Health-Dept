Help Page: Who Should Apply

This tie-up program is open to:

  • Accredited Nursing Colleges offering Diploma and Degree Programs in Nursing
  • ANM (Auxiliary Nurse Midwifery) Training Institutes
  • Medical Colleges that wish to collaborate with public health centers for clinical practice, training, and research opportunities

If you belong to any of these categories and wish to establish a partnership, please follow the application process outlined in this guide.
